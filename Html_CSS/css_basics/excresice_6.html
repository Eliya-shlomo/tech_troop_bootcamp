My Pasta recipe

Ingredients

  • 500 gr of your favorite pasta
  • garlic cloves
  • 400 gr of mushed tomato
  • 2 spoons of olive oil
  • A handful of basil
  • salt,paper and bit of sugar

Preparation steps

  1. put the pasta on boiled and salted water - salted like the sea
  2. fried the garlic until he got carmelized
  3. pull the tomato sauce to the pan with the garlic
  4. Seasoning with love
  5. take the paste out of the water, cook with the tomato sauce for 1 minutes, put the basil for on the end
